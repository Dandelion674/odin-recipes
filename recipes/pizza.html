Homemade Pizza

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners,
you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim
and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame. Learn how to make the best pizza
crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough,
and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F to 115째F)
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add 2 cups of flour, olive oil, salt, and sugar to the yeast mixture. Stir until combined.
  3. Gradually add more flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  7. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  8. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!

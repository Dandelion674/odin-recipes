Hotdog

Description

Hotdogs are a classic American food, perfect for barbecues, picnics, and quick meals.
They are easy to prepare and can be customized with a variety of toppings.
Whether grilled, boiled, or steamed, hotdogs are a favorite for many.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog buns
  • Hotdogs (beef, pork, or turkey)
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Onions (diced)
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil or grill the hotdogs according to your preference.
  2. While the hotdogs are cooking, prepare the buns by toasting them lightly.
  3. Once the hotdogs are cooked, place each one in a bun.
  4. Add your favorite toppings such as ketchup, mayo, diced onions, and cheese.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious hotdogs!

Burger

Description

Burgers are a classic American dish, perfect for grilling and gatherings.
They can be customized with a variety of toppings and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or plant-based alternative)
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, add a slice on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your choice of sauces on top.
  9. Cover with the top half of the bun.
  10. Serve immediately with your favorite sides like fries or salad.
